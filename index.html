Be Fare to BMCC Students

K-12 students in NYC are given free access to public transportation… but loose it upon graduating high school. BMCC students also need this support to be able to attend their classes and reach their educational goals.

Brandon's Story

We were inspired to tackle this issue by one of our team members and fellow BMCC student, Brandon: “As a Queens resident, commuting to and from school is time consuming and expensive for me. I need to take two trains and usually need at least 1.5 hours to get to my destination. Given that I do this commute 5 days a week, my weekly expenses are at least $40/week. An expansion of the free OMNY system (that is already in place for grades K-12 in NYC) would be substantial.”
